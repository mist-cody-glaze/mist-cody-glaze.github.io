How to Send an Email in Gmail

By Cody Glaze


Step 1: Select "Compose" Icon

After you have logged into your gmail account, click on the "Compose" in the top left hand corner of your screen. When you do this, an email composing window will populate.


Step 2: Type Email Address of Recipients

There are several different ways to send an email to a recipient. Each method has some aspects to consider.

  1. TO:
    • Email is directly intended for the recipient
    • All "To" recipients can reply to all "To" and "CC" recipients.
    • All "To" recipients can see all other recipient email addresses.

  2. Carbon Copy (CC):
    • Email is intended for recipients that are indirectly involved in the conversation but should be included.
    • All "CC" recipients can reply to all "To" and "CC" recipients.
    • All "CC" recipients can see all other recipient email addresses.

  3. Blind Carbon Copy (BCC):
    • Email is intended for recipients that are indirectly involved in the conversation but should be included.
    • "BCC" recipients can only see and reply to the original sender's email.


Step 3: Give Your Email a Subject Title

It is important to give your email a subject title. Subject titles are intended to give the recipient an idea of the email's content.

Subject titles should be brief yet descriptive in nature. For example, if the email content revolves around a recent meeting, the subject title could be "Thurday's Meeting".


Step 4: Compose the Body of Your Email

The contents of your email are placed in the "Body" section. Follow these steps to compose the body of your email:

  1. First, address your recipient(s) with a greeting. Examples include: Dear [Name], Hello [Name], To Whom it May Concern, etc.

  2. Next, type the indtended message you want to communicate with recipients

  3. Lastly, end your email with a sign-off and your name. Examples include: Sincerely, Best, Regards, etc. This would also be the section to include information such as your job title and contact information

  4. In addition, you can customize your email's style and layout with editing options located toward the bottom of window. You can use these features to change the font and text size, create numbered lists, adjust spacing, etc.

Step 5: Add Additional Items

The final and optional step is to include additional items necessary in your email. Depending on the purpose of the email, this could include a website link, a digital file attachment, a picture, a calendar invitation, etc.


Step 6: Send Your Email

The last and final step is to send your email. To do this, simply click on the "Send" button

Keep in mind, once you send the email, you cannot unsend it. Double check that your email conveys an appropriate message and neccessary content for the intended audience, and check for spelling and grammar mistakes.
